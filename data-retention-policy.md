# Data & Privacy Policy — Hotel Capital Stack Diagnostic

**Modern Hospitality Solutions (MHS)** · Plain-English version

We built this tool for people in sensitive financial situations. So privacy is a feature, not a footnote. Here is exactly how your information is handled.

## The short version

- You can use this tool **without entering your property name**.
- You can **skip any sensitive question** — exact number, rough range, estimate, "I don't know," or "prefer not to say" are all allowed.
- Your answers stay **in your browser**. By default nothing is uploaded or sent anywhere.
- Your report clearly shows which answers were **estimated, ranged, or missing**.
- You can **delete everything** with one click at any time.

## Where your data lives

This version of the tool runs entirely in your web browser (client-side). When you type a number, it is held in your browser's memory to do the math and show your results. It is **not transmitted to MHS, to any server, or to any third party**, and it is **never used to train any AI model**.

### Session-only by default
If you do nothing, your answers exist only for the current session. Close or refresh the tab and they are gone.

### Optional "save on this browser"
In the Privacy panel you can opt in to **save your progress on this browser**. This stores your answers locally on your own device (using the browser's local storage). It is still never uploaded. It lets you return later and pick up where you left off. You can turn this off or delete it at any time.

### One-click delete
The Privacy panel has a **Delete all my data** button. It removes any locally saved answers and resets your session immediately.

## Anonymous mode

Anonymous mode means the tool will not ask for, store, or display a property name anywhere — including on the downloadable memo. The basic diagnostic never requires a property name. A name is optional and is used only to label a report if you choose to add one.

## Known, estimated, and assumed values

The tool separates three kinds of inputs and shows them clearly:

- **Known** — you entered an exact figure.
- **Estimated / range** — you provided an approximation; treated as directional.
- **Assumed** — you left something blank, so the tool used a conservative, clearly logged default (for example, a refinance rate range or a target DSCR range).

Every assumption appears in the **Assumptions used** section of your results and in your memo. The tool never hides an assumption.

## Reports and exports

- No report is created until you choose to generate one.
- Reports are generated locally in your browser (download or print/Save-as-PDF).
- Every report carries the watermark: *"Prepared from user-provided and estimated information."*
- A result is labeled **not lender-ready** until the required inputs are confirmed, so a rough draft is never mistaken for a finished underwriting.

## What we do not do

- We do not sell your data.
- We do not use your inputs or uploaded documents to train models.
- We do not publish or benchmark your data publicly without your explicit consent.
- We do not make a lending decision or commitment — this is a diagnostic and planning tool.

## If this is hosted as a service later

This prototype is client-side only. If MHS later offers a hosted version with accounts and saved scenarios, that version will add: encryption in transit and at rest, a documented data-usage policy, the ability to delete your account and data, secure file upload, and a session-only option where technically feasible. Those features are planned for a future version and are described here so the commitment is on the record.

## Not advice

This tool is a diagnostic and planning aid only. It is not financial, legal, tax, lending, or investment advice and is not a lending commitment. Final decisions should involve your lender, CPA, attorney, broker, or capital advisor as appropriate.
